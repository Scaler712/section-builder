FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot.

How long does migration take? +

Most teams complete their migration in under a weekend. Our CLI handles the heavy lifting, and our migration guides cover every major framework. Enterprise customers get a dedicated migration engineer.

Is there a free tier? +

Yes. The Starter plan is free forever with 100K requests per month, 1 GB storage, and edge deploys. No credit card required. You only pay when you scale past the free limits.

What about vendor lock-in? +

Zero lock-in. We use standard Docker containers and open formats. You can export your entire configuration and migrate away at any time. We earn your business every month.

Do you support my framework? +

If it runs in a container, it runs on Vertex. We have first-class support for Next.js, Nuxt, Rails, Django, Go, Rust, and anything else you throw at us. Custom buildpacks available on Pro and above.

What happens during traffic spikes? +

Auto scaling kicks in within 200ms. We pre-warm instances based on traffic patterns, so most spikes are handled before you even notice. You will never get a surprise bill either — we alert you before overages.

Is SOC 2 compliance included? +

SOC 2 Type II is included on all Enterprise plans. Pro plans include our security whitepaper and data processing agreement. We undergo annual third-party audits and publish the results.
