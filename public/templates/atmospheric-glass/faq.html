Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How quickly can I get set up?

+

Most users launch their first campaign within 30 minutes of signing up. Our onboarding wizard walks you through everything, and pre-built templates mean you're never starting from a blank page.

Do I need any technical or design skills?

+

Not at all. The platform was designed for non-technical founders and marketers. Everything is drag-and-drop, and our AI handles the optimization automatically. If you can write an email, you can use this.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

+

You simply choose a plan that fits your needs. If it's not for you, cancel with one click -- no calls, no retention teams, no tricks. We'll even export your data for you. No hard feelings.

Can I switch plans later?

+

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences. No penalties, ever.

Will this integrate with my existing tools?

+

Yes. We integrate natively with Stripe, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Zapier, Google Analytics, and 50+ other tools. Our API is also available on Professional and Enterprise plans for custom integrations.

What kind of support do you offer?

+

Starter gets email support (24hr response). Professional gets priority support with a 4-hour response guarantee. Enterprise gets a dedicated account manager plus Slack channel access with our team.
