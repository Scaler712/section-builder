FAQ

Questions & Answers

How quickly can I get started?

+
Most teams are fully onboarded within 48 hours. We provide migration tools that pull your existing data from any platform, and our setup wizard handles the rest. No engineering time required.

Do I need a technical background?

+
Not at all. The platform is designed for operators, not engineers. Everything is visual and template-based. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can use this. Our most successful users are marketers and founders.

What integrations do you support?

+
Over 200 native integrations including all major ad platforms, CRMs, email tools, analytics suites, and payment processors. If it has an API, we can connect to it. Custom webhooks are also supported.

Is my data secure?

+
SOC 2 Type II certified with end-to-end encryption. Your data is stored in region-specific data centers with automatic backups. We never sell or share your data, and you can export or delete everything at any time.

What happens if I cancel?

+
Cancel anytime with two clicks. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your billing cycle, and you can export all your data before you leave. We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee.

Do you offer custom enterprise plans?

+
Yes. Enterprise plans include dedicated infrastructure, custom SLAs, SSO, advanced RBAC, and a dedicated account manager. Contact our sales team for a tailored proposal based on your team's specific requirements.
