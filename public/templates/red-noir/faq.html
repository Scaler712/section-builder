FAQ

Got questions. We have answers.

How fast do I get my first batch? +

72 hours from onboarding. We start with a strategy session, pull your existing data, and deliver your first set of tested variations within 3 business days.

Do you manage our ad accounts? +

No. We are a creative engine, not a media buying agency. We build and optimize the ads — you or your media buyer runs them. We integrate directly with your team via Slack and shared dashboards.

What formats do you deliver? +

9:16 for TikTok and Reels, 1:1 for feed, 16:9 for YouTube pre-roll. We deliver platform-ready assets with proper safe zones, text overlays, and captions baked in.

What if the ads do not perform? +

We iterate until they do. Our process is built on rapid testing. If a concept does not work, we analyze why, adjust the variables, and deliver new variations within 48 hours. We are not done until your ROAS targets are met.

Is there a minimum contract? +

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month on all plans. We earn your business every 30 days. That said, our average client stays for 11 months because the results compound over time.

How is this different from UGC agencies? +

UGC agencies send you raw creator content and hope it works. We use AI-generated performance creative combined with systematic hook testing, CTA optimization, and visual sequence analysis. Every asset is built to convert, not just to look native.
