Common questions

We're here to help

Everything you're wondering about, answered with care.

How much time does this take each day?

+

As little as 10 minutes. Our meditations start at 5 minutes, and the recipes take under 30 minutes. We designed everything for real people with busy lives -- not for someone with unlimited free time.

I've never meditated before. Is this for me?

+

Absolutely. Our guided sessions are designed specifically for beginners. No experience needed, no special equipment. Just you, a quiet moment, and a gentle voice guiding you through.

What if I don't like it?

+

No problem at all. You have 30 days to try everything risk-free. If it doesn't feel right, simply let us know and we'll refund you completely. No questions, no guilt, no awkward conversations.

Are the recipes suitable for dietary restrictions?

+

Yes. Every recipe includes tags for gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, and nut-free options. You can filter the entire library by your dietary needs. We believe nourishment should never feel restrictive.

Can I do this while pregnant or postpartum?

+

Many of our members are pregnant or postpartum. We have specific meditation tracks and recipe guides for each stage. That said, always check with your healthcare provider before starting any new wellness program.

How is the community moderated?

+

We take community safety seriously. Our space is actively moderated by trained facilitators. We have clear guidelines around respect, kindness, and confidentiality. It's a judgment-free zone, always.
