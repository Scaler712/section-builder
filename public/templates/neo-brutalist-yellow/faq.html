Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need any technical skills to use this?

+
Absolutely not. If you can write an email, you can build a page. Our drag-and-drop editor handles all the technical stuff behind the scenes. No coding, no design skills, no headaches.

Can I use my own domain name?

+
Yes. Custom domains are included on the Growth and Agency plans. You can connect any domain you own in about 5 minutes. We handle the SSL certificate automatically.

What happens if I go over my visitor limit?

+
Your pages stay live. We'll send you a heads-up when you're at 80% of your limit, and you can upgrade at any time. We never take your pages down mid-campaign.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?

+
No contracts, no commitments. You can cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard with one click. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

+
Yes. Every plan comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, email us and we'll refund your payment. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through.

Can I integrate with my existing email tool?

+
We integrate with all major email platforms including Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, and 40+ others. Plus we have a Zapier integration for anything else you might use.
