STUCK
Sound Familiar?

You Keep Hitting
the Same Walls

  • 01

    You consume endlessly but never implement. Another podcast, another course, another saved bookmark you'll never revisit. Knowledge without action is just entertainment.

  • 02

    You're paralyzed by too many options. Every guru says something different. SaaS, agency, course, freelancing. The sheer volume of paths freezes you before you start.

  • 03

    Programs that overpromise and underdeliver. Paid $997 for a course that was mostly hype and filler. Got a Slack invite and some PDFs. Didn't move the needle.

  • 04

    Zero accountability structure. When motivation fades, nobody pushes you forward. No deadlines, no stakes, no one to answer to but yourself.

  • 05

    You feel behind everyone else. Watching peers announce wins while you're still figuring out fundamentals. The gap feels wider every month.

  • 06

    Your tools don't talk to each other. Spreadsheets, Notion, Slack, email. Frankensteined together with duct tape. Nothing is centralized, nothing is automated.
